Our Story

Founded in February 2022 at Bengaluru, Swasthya Food Consulting was created with a vision to provide world-class consultation to the food industry. In today's landscape, food safety is not optional — it is vital. The country's apex body, FSSAI, drives rigorous regulations across the food sector, and navigating those demands expertise, precision, and integrity.

Swasthya is a one-stop solution for food manufacturers and food establishments — covering quality systems, compliance, technical support, and professional training — all under one roof.

Our Narrative Journey

Key Milestones of Excellence

2022
Chapter I: The Genesis

A Vision for World-Class Safety

Swasthya wasn't just born in a boardroom; it was born from a realization. In Bengaluru, Mr. Thammaiah ND saw a critical gap between local operations and global safety standards. He established Swasthya Food Consulting with a singular mission: to bridge this gap and bring global-tier food safety to every corner of the Indian industry.

2022
Chapter II: Anchoring Trust

QCI & APEDA Recognition

Trust is earned with precision. Our QCI and APEDA registrations weren't just administrative hurdles—they were our handshake with the industry. These certifications validated our national caliber and promised a level of consultant-led excellence that manufacturers across India could finally rely on with total confidence.

2023
Chapter III: Educational Revolution

FoSTaC Academy Leadership

Knowledge is the highest form of safety. By becoming an authorized FoSTaC training center and ranking as the **#1 Milk Products Trainer nationally**, we empowered the next generation of food safety professionals. Our curriculum moved beyond basic rules, teaching the technical sovereignty needed to lead in the modern market.

2024
Chapter IV: Global Sovereignty

A Legacy of Global Impact

Today, Swasthya is a beacon of global authority. Partnerships with BRCGS as an Approved Training Partner (ATP) and EHEDG mean our clients aren't just compliant—they are industry leaders. With 135+ successful track records, we continue to redefine what 'safe food' means, creating impact that spans borders and generations.
