Our Story

Built on expertise.
Driven by purpose.

Swasthya Food Consulting (Est. 2022) was founded with a paramount vision: to deliver world-class consultancy & training services to the global food industry. In an era where consumer trust is of utmost importance, maintaining rigorous food safety standards is not just a regulatory requirement - it is a moral and operational imperative.

With each passing year, our country’s apex food authority - Food Safety and Standards Authority of India (FSSAI) is stringently driving the regulations required for food industry and food service establishments. This is going to be more stringent in coming days.

Hence, it is the vision of Swasthya Food Consulting to serve as a strategic partner, guiding organizations through the complexities of compliance and quality assurance. Under the eminent leadership of the company’s founder, we provide a comprehensive, "one-stop solution" designed to streamline operations for food manufacturers and food service establishments alike.

Few reasons why we think of immense help to our clients are:

  • Deep Industry Expertise: Decades of experience in multisectoral quality, food safety and regulatory.
  • Regulatory Precision: Specialized knowledge in FSSAI standards, regulations and global standards.
  • Tailored Solutions: Customized frameworks that fit the unique needs of your food business.
  • Customer-Centric Approach: A focus on sustainable growth through quality assurance.

The Expert Behind the Firm

Mr. Thammaiah ND

Managing Director

Mr. Thammaiah ND, a Master Graduate in Dairy Science (M. Tech) with over 22 years of global expertise is the Founder and Managing director of our company.

Thammaiah’s distinguished career includes a decade in Saudi Arabia at premier organizations such as Almarai (World’s largest vertically integrated Dairy company) and NADEC.

He is one of the top 100 performing FoSTaC Trainer in India and holds top performer FoSTaC Trainer on Advance Manufacturing - Milk and Milk Products Course. He is also BRCGS Approved Training Partner (ATP) for Food Safety Courses across the globe.

He is FoSTaC Trainer for Advance Manufacturing – Milk and Milk Products, Advance Manufacturing, Advance Catering, Advance Storage & Transport, Advance Retail & Distribution, Basic Catering, Basic Retail & Distribution and Street Food Vending.

He is one of the renowned BRCGS and Food Safety Consultant in India and successfully consulted more than 150+ food industry and food service establishments across the country.

As of April 2026, Swasthya Food Consulting partners with more than 135 leading organizations by bridging the gap between international best practices and local regulatory requirements, the firm provides all the necessary support to clients with the strategic framework necessary to achieve world-class food safety and operational excellence.
