The Spectrum of Global Authority

Tailored implementation of GFSI-benchmarked certifications for international authority.

ISO 22000:2018

ISO 22000 Consultancy

This global standard integrates HACCP principles with ISO’s management structure to manage food safety risks. Implementation involves identifying physical, chemical, and biological hazards throughout the food chain.

FSSC 22000 v6

FSSC 22000 Consultancy

FSSC 22000 provides a complete certification scheme for food safety systems, incorporating ISO 22000, sector-specific PRPs, and additional requirements. Recognized by the Global Food Safety Initiative (GFSI).

BRCGS Food Safety

BRC-Food Consultancy

The British Retail Consortium (BRC) Global Standard for Food Safety focuses on product safety, integrity, and quality. Implementation requires rigorous adherence to environmental standards and process controls.

ISO 9001:2015

ISO 9001:2015 Consultancy

Establishing a robust Quality Management System (QMS) centered on risk-based thinking and the PDCA cycle. Implementation helps organizations streamline processes and enhance customer satisfaction.

BRCGS Packaging

BRC-Packaging Consultancy

Ensures packaging materials do not compromise food safety. Implementation involves assessing hygiene of manufacturing environments and chemical migration risks into food.

AIB International

AIB Standards Consultancy

Focuses heavily on sanitary design and operational excellence. Implementation centers on rigorous inspection protocols, pest control, and cleaning practices for manufacturers.
